Welcome to my digital garden :) 

I'm a 20-ish year old mechatronics student from Germany studying at the [KIT](https://kit.edu). This is my blog, personal dumping ground (for knowledge of course), and just a general place to share my thoughts.  

Feel free to send me a hello at digital-garden@rennersh.de. You can visit my normal Website [here](https://rennersh.de). You can read the [[Garden/About me\|About me]] if you want to know more about me, for whatever reason.

## What is a digital garden?
A digital garden is a way to loosely organize ideas and writings, more a graph than a blog, to share publicly with the world. The idea is not to publish perfect pieces of writing but to slowly evolve and grow a library of rough and unfinished ideas as well as shareable knowledge over time. It has become a lot more popular over the last few years, especially since the release of [Obsidian](https://obsidian.md) with which this digital garden is also maintained.
